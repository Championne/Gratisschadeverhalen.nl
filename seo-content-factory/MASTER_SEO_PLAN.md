## WHY WE DON'T COPY EVERYTHING FROM 112SCHADE.NL

### Important Context for This Strategy

112schade.nl has been building their website for **10+ years** and offers services we do not provide. 
This section explains why we take a **focused approach** rather than copying their entire site.

### Reason 1: Focus Beats Breadth

112schade.nl offers services we don't provide:
- Lease Verhaalservice (complex B2B fleet management)
- Loonregres (employer wage recovery claims)
- Opstal/inboedel/aansprakelijkheid (property insurance disputes)
- Direct letselschade handling (we refer to Unitas)

**If we copied everything, 50% would be irrelevant** and confuse our brand positioning.
Visitors would click on services we can't deliver ‚Üí bad experience ‚Üí no conversion.

### Reason 2: Quality Over Quantity

By focusing on **40 high-quality articles about materi√´le schade** instead of 60 articles about everything:
- We go **deeper** on each topic
- We build **stronger topical authority** in our niche
- We rank **higher** for keywords that actually convert to customers
- Each article gets more attention and better optimization

### Reason 3: Modern SEO Rewards Expertise (E-E-A-T)

Google's algorithm favors sites that demonstrate **deep expertise in a focused area** over sites that cover everything superficially.

| Our Positioning | Their Positioning |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| "The experts in gratis materi√´le autoschade verhalen" | "We do everything (lease, letsel, opstal, etc.)" |
| Focused authority | Broad but shallow |
| Clear value proposition | Complex offering |

### Reason 4: Conversion Optimization

Every page we create has ONE goal: **get people to /claim-indienen**

If we added lease, letsel, opstal pages:
- Visitors get confused about what we actually do
- They click on services we can't deliver
- They have a worse experience
- Conversion rate drops
- We waste marketing spend

### Conclusion

**We copy the STRUCTURE and SEO STRATEGY of 112schade.nl, but only for services we actually offer.**
This gives us the best of both worlds: proven SEO tactics + focused positioning.
