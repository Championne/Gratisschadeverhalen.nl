# Rebranding Plan: autoschadebureau.nl to 112autoschade.nl

## 1. Project Overview and Goals

### 1.1 Project Goal
To successfully rebrand "autoschadebureau.nl" to "112autoschade.nl", establishing a new, memorable, and legally sound brand identity, while maintaining and enhancing existing search engine visibility and customer trust.

### 1.2 Key Objectives
*   Acquire and secure the new domain "112autoschade.nl".
*   Seamless migration of website content and functionality.
*   Update all brand assets (logo, name, copy) across digital and physical touchpoints.
*   Ensure legal compliance for the new brand name.
*   Communicate the rebrand effectively to all stakeholders.
*   Minimize negative impact on SEO and organic traffic.
*   Update internal systems and email infrastructure.

## 2. Technical Implementation

### 2.1 Domain Acquisition and Redirection Strategy

#### 2.1.1 Domain Acquisition
*   **Action:** Secure "112autoschade.nl" and any relevant variations (.com, .net, etc., if applicable/desired).
*   **Responsibility:** IT/Project Lead
*   **Timeline:** Week 1

#### 2.1.2 301 Redirects (Crucial for SEO)
*   **Action:** Implement permanent (301) redirects from every URL on "autoschadebureau.nl" to its corresponding URL on "112autoschade.nl".
    *   Example: `autoschadebureau.nl/contact` -> `112autoschade.nl/contact`
*   **Prioritization:** Redirect all pages, especially high-traffic and indexed pages.
*   **Monitoring:** Regularly check redirect health post-launch.
*   **Responsibility:** IT/Web Development
*   **Timeline:** Week 2-3 (pre-launch testing)

#### 2.1.3 Internal Linking Update
*   **Action:** Update all internal links on the new `112autoschade.nl` website to point to the new domain.
*   **Responsibility:** Web Development/SEO
*   **Timeline:** Week 3

### 2.2 Website Content Updates

#### 2.2.1 Logo and Branding
*   **Action:** Design and implement the new "112autoschade.nl" logo across the entire website.
*   **Responsibility:** Design/Web Development
*   **Timeline:** Week 2

#### 2.2.2 Name and Copy
*   **Action:** Review all website text, including:
    *   Company name mentions
    *   About Us page
    *   Service descriptions
    *   Contact information
    *   Legal disclaimers (privacy policy, terms of service)
    *   Call-to-actions
*   **Action:** Update all instances to reflect "112autoschade.nl" and the new brand messaging.
*   **Responsibility:** Marketing/Content Team
*   **Timeline:** Week 2-3

#### 2.2.3 Favicon Update
*   **Action:** Update the website favicon to align with the new brand.
*   **Responsibility:** Web Development
*   **Timeline:** Week 2

## 3. Legal Considerations

### 3.1 Trademark Search and Registration
*   **Action:** Conduct a thorough trademark search for "112autoschade.nl" to ensure availability and avoid infringement issues. (Consider Benelux/EU registration).
*   **Action:** Initiate trademark registration process once cleared.
*   **Responsibility:** Legal Counsel/Management
*   **Timeline:** Week 1-4

### 3.2 Business Registration Update
*   **Action:** Update company registration details with the Chamber of Commerce (KvK) to reflect the new brand name (if the legal entity name changes, or for trade name registration).
*   **Responsibility:** Legal Counsel/Management
*   **Timeline:** Week 4-6

### 3.3 Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy
*   **Action:** Review and update existing T&Cs and Privacy Policy to reflect the new brand name and any other necessary legal amendments.
*   **Responsibility:** Legal Counsel
*   **Timeline:** Week 3-4
